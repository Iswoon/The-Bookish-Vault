₰ The Tome of Recommendations ₰

The Name of the Wind

Author: Patrick Rothfuss

A lyrical tale of magic, music, and myth — the life of Kvothe, told in his own voice.

A Darker Shade of Magic

Author: V.E. Schwab

Parallel Londons, dangerous magic, and a coat with many worlds sewn into its folds.

Mistborn

Author: Brandon Sanderson

A world ruled by ash and an immortal tyrant, where rebellion burns in secret.

Uprooted

Author: Naomi Novik

A lush, fairy-tale-infused fantasy about a quiet village, an ancient forest, and a reluctant girl chosen by a reclusive wizard known as the Dragon.

The Priory of the Orange Tree

Author: Samantha Shannon

A sweeping epic of dragons, queens, and ancient prophecies. A world divided must unite or fall to shadow and fire.

The Stardust Thief

Author: Chelsea Abdullah

A tale inspired by One Thousand and One Nights — full of djinn, desert magic, and stories that twist like smoke under starlight.
